Established in 2009 with the vision of ‘Health and Joy for All’, Swasth Foundation is on a mission to build a sustainable and accountable health ecosystem. The organisation’s network of primary healthcare centres provides affordable services to reduce out-of-pocket expenditure by over 50% for those in need. The centres are focused on enhancing the state of the physical, social, mental and emotional well-being of people. This is done through interventions like courses, classes, workshops and one-on-one interactions. read more

Swasthya Swaraj Society deals With healthcare and education as their areas of focus, this nonprofit is working towards making good health a reality for the poorest and the underserved. It has set up model community health programmes in tribal areas like Thuamul Rampur Block of Kalahandi district in Odisha, where families are destitute and their children grossly undernourished. The tribals living here are too familiar with deprivation, sicknesses, and deaths – many unreported and from preventable causes. read more

The Gautam Gambhir Foundation is extending their support to the marginalized, by assuring that their health care workers stand at the first line of defense.
